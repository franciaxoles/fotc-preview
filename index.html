Who We Are

Family, faith, and a future — in Trujillo, Honduras

Farm of the Child USA is a Catholic non-profit that supports Finca del Niño-Honduras — a mission modeled on the Holy Family that educates, protects, and promotes healing for vulnerable children and families in the community of Trujillo, on Honduras's Garífuna coast.

"The children are not orphans — they are family." — Vincent Pescatore, Founder

Founded over 30 years ago by Vincent and Zulena Pescatore, Finca del Niño has grown into a thriving community where children, lay missionaries, and local families come together around a shared mission of love.

Our Full Story → What We Do →
✝

For over 30 years, Finca del Niño has been a beacon on the Garífuna coast — providing 20 resident children with a loving home staffed by lay missionaries, access to healthcare, spiritual formation, education, and daily living necessities.
